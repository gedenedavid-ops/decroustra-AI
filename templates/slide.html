Missing Persons — Côte d'Ivoire
TRACK 3 · SECURITY & COMPLIANCE

🔴 Problem

In Côte d'Ivoire, missing person alerts are scattered across dozens of Facebook groups. Zero centralization. Families manually scan hundreds of posts daily.

🟢 Solution

AI agent that scrapes Facebook public groups, extracts missing persons automatically, and lets families search in natural language: "My son David, 11, Abidjan?"

⚠️ What it does NOT do

• Does not replace emergency services (Police 170, Fire 185)
• No legal or medical advice · No direct family contact
• Public data only — accuracy cannot be 100% guaranteed
